    I am a passionate Unity Developer who believes that great games are built on solid logic, not just tools.

    My career took a defining turn after a technical interview where I realized I had become too reliant on AI. That moment was my wake-up call. I went back to the roots of programming, sharpening my C# logic and problem-solving skills to ensure I am the architect of my code. Today, I build complex, scalable systems in Unity with confidence.

    My approach is balanced: I use AI 60% of the time to accelerate code generation and implementation, but I maintain 40% control over the logic flow to ensure the desired outcome. I reference best practices, design patterns, and proven solutions, then integrate them strategically to build robust, maintainable systems. This way, I leverage AI's efficiency while staying in command of the architecture and critical decision-making.

    I also love bridging the gap between games and apps. I originally prototyped my VegCal tool in Godot, but to deliver a premium user experience, I taught myself Kotlin and rebuilt it natively in Android Studio - Shop Calculator. I used AI to accelerate learning this new language, successfully implementing Cloud Saving (Drive API) and Monetization.

    Currently, I am pushing my boundaries by exploring Unreal Engine 5 while seeking new challenges in professional Unity Game Development.
